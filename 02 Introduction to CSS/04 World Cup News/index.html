Euro Cup News

The UEFA European Championship, also known as the Euros, is a quadrennial international football tournament contested by the men's national teams of member associations of the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA). It's considered one of the most prestigious football tournaments in the world, alongside the FIFA World Cup.

The first edition took place in 1960 with just four teams participating. Since then, the tournament has grown significantly, with the most recent edition in 2020 featuring 24 teams. Each edition is hosted by a different European country, and the winner receives the Henri Delaunay trophy.

The Euros are filled with excitement and drama. Top European players battle it out for national glory, creating unforgettable moments for fans. The competition is fierce, with many strong contenders vying for the title.
